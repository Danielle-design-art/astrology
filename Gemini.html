Gemini

Gemini, the air sign represented by twins, is known for being intellectually curious, adaptable, and communicative. They are often described as social butterflies, quick-witted, and enjoy intellectual stimulation. However, Geminis can also struggle with indecisiveness and restlessness.

  • 3rd sign of Zodiac
  • Mutable Air Sign
  • Ruling Planet: Mercury
