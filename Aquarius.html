Aquarius

Aquarians are known for their innovative, intellectual, and humanitarian traits, often described as forward-thinking, unique, and independent. They are also seen as progressive, rebellious, and have a strong sense of justice. Aquarians are often creative and enjoy mental stimulation.

  • 11th sign of Zodiac
  • Fixed Air Sign
  • Ruling Planet: Uranus
