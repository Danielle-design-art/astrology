♋ Cancer the Crab

Cancers are known for their nurturing, intuitive, and emotional nature, with a strong sense of home and family. They are also protective and loyal, often described as being reserved but fiercely dedicated to those they love. While they are known for their empathy and generosity, they can also be moody and sensitive, retreating into their shells when hurt or upset.

  • 4th sign of Zodiac
  • Cardinal Water Sign
  • Ruling Planet: Moon
  • Colors:Pale colors, cream, white
