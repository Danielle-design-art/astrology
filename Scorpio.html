Scorpio the Scorpion

Scorpios are known for their intense, passionate, and magnetic personalities. They are deeply emotional, intuitive, and often secretive, with a strong sense of loyalty and a knack for reading people. Their ambition, confidence, and determination make them natural leaders, and they are often drawn to the darker, more mysterious aspects of life.

  • 8th sign of Zodiac
  • Fixed Water Sign
  • Ruling Planet: Mars/Pluto
  • Colors:Black, blood red, charcoal grey
