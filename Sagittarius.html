Sagittarius the Archer

Sagittarius personalities are known for their adventurous, optimistic, and philosophical nature, often seeking new experiences and knowledge. They are energetic, free-spirited, and enjoy a good laugh. While they can be impatient and blunt, they are also generous and have a strong desire for freedom.
