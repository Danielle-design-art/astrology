♓ Pisces the Fish

Pisces individuals are known for their deep empathy, intuition, and creativity, often described as the "dreamers" of the zodiac. They are highly sensitive and attuned to the emotions of others, making them compassionate and supportive friends and partners. However, this sensitivity can also lead to a tendency to be overly trusting and easily hurt.

  • 12th sign of Zodiac
  • Mutable Water Sign
  • Ruling Planet: Neptune
  • Colors:Muave, purple, aquamarine
