♈ Aries the Ram

Aries individuals are known for their assertive, energetic, and courageous nature, often described as passionate, enthusiastic, and ambitious. They are natural leaders, enjoy taking initiative, and have a strong desire to be the best at what they do. While they can be impatient and impulsive, their fiery temperament and unwavering determination can also make them successful and inspiring.

  • 1st sign of Zodiac
  • Cardinal Fire Sign
  • Ruling Planet: Mars
  • Colors:All Shades of Red
