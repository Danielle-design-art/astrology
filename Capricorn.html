Capricorn the Goat

Capricorns are known for their practical, grounded, and disciplined nature, making them highly ambitious and hardworking. They are often seen as responsible and dedicated to achieving their goals, and can be quite methodical and organized in their approach to life. While they are often reserved emotionally, they are also fiercely loyal and deeply committed to those they love.

  • 10th sign of Zodiac
  • Cardinal Earth Sign
  • Ruling Planet: Saturn
  • Colors:All shades brown,orange, black
