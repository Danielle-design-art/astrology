Libra

Libras are known for their diplomatic and charming personalities, a strong sense of justice, and a desire for balance and harmony. They are often described as fair-minded, compassionate, and adaptable. However, they can also struggle with indecisiveness and a tendency to avoid conflict.

  • 7th sign of Zodiac
  • Cardinal Air Sign
  • Ruling Planet: Venus
